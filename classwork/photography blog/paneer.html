Your Paneer Kohlapuri Recipe

A typical Maharashtrian taste profile, with very rich, complex spice notes, perfect with a crispy parantha or steamed rice, along with some amti dal or kachumber.

Total time: 50-55 minutes

Ingredients

  • Coconut: 1 cup (70 gms approx.), grated
  • Dry Red Chillies: 4 pieces
  • Onion: 2, finely chopped
  • Paneer: 750 gms, cut into cubes
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. In a karahi, dry roast grated coconut, cloves, cinnamon, whole pepper, red chillies, poppy seeds, cumin seeds and coriander seeds.

    Time: 10 minutes

  2. Heat oil in a karahi and saut√© the onions till soft and translucent, add ginger-garlic paste and green chillies. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala and salt.

    Time: 10 minutes

  3. Add the coarsely ground spices, mix well and fry for 5-10 minutes. Add water, paneer. Cover and let it simmer for 5 minutes. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

    Time: 20 minutes
